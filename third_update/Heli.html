Heli GF-2

Capacity

The GF-2 business helicopter is designed for comfortable transportation of up to 6 passengers, which makes it an excellent choice for business and private trips. The compact but spacious cabin provides enough space for convenient accommodation of passengers and their luggage.

Power

The GF-2 is equipped with a single 900 horsepower turboshaft engine. This powerful engine provides high performance and reliability, allowing the helicopter to reach a cruising speed of up to 250 km/h and reach a flight range of up to 700 km. The efficient engine design guarantees economical fuel consumption and minimal emissions.

Interior design

The interior of the GF-2 is designed with an emphasis on comfort and functionality. The leather seats provide a high level of comfort, and the adjustable lighting allows you to create the perfect atmosphere for work or leisure. The helicopter is equipped with a modern air conditioning system that maintains an optimal microclimate on board.

Heli TR-7

Capacity

The TR-7 is designed to carry up to 8 passengers, offering a spacious and comfortable cabin. This helicopter is ideal for corporate travel and private travel, providing ample space for all passengers and their luggage.

Power

The TR-7 is equipped with two turboshaft engines with a total capacity of 1,800 horsepower. These engines provide high speed and reliability, allowing the helicopter to reach a cruising speed of up to 280 km/h and reach a flight range of up to 800 km. The dual engine system improves flight safety and stability in all weather conditions.

Interior design

The interior of the TR-7 is made using premium materials and modern technologies. The spacious interior is equipped with comfortable leather chairs with the possibility of adjustment, which allows passengers to find the optimal position for maximum comfort.

Heli QT-9

Capacity

The QT-9 is designed to carry up to 12 passengers, making it an ideal choice for large delegations and group trips. The spacious cabin provides comfortable accommodation for all passengers and their luggage, which is especially important for long-haul flights.

Power

The QT-9 is equipped with two turboshaft engines with a total output of 2,500 horsepower. These powerful engines provide high performance and reliability, allowing the helicopter to reach cruising speeds of up to 300 km/h and reach a flight range of up to 1,000 km. The powerful engine system ensures stable operation and safety even in difficult flight conditions.

Interior design

The interior of the QT-9 is designed in a luxury style using high-quality materials and the latest technologies. Spacious leather armchairs with adjustment and heating function provide maximum comfort. The helicopter is equipped with modern multimedia systems, including individual screens and entertainment systems.
