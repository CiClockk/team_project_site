Jet UF-25

Capacity

The UF-25 business aircraft is designed for comfortable transportation of up to 8 passengers. The spacious cabin provides enough space for all passengers, making this aircraft ideal for business and personal trips over short and medium distances.

Power

The UF-25 is equipped with two turbojet engines, each of which provides thrust up to 4,000 pounds. These powerful engines allow the aircraft to reach cruising speeds of up to 800 km/h and cover distances of up to 4,000 km without refueling. The efficient design of the engines ensures low fuel consumption and minimal emissions, which makes flights not only fast, but also economical.

Interior design

The interior of the UF-25 is designed with an emphasis on comfort and functionality. Leather seats that can be adjusted for maximum comfort, large windows that provide excellent lighting and visibility, as well as modern entertainment systems create a cozy and productive atmosphere on board.

Jet LP-5

Capacity

The LP-5 is designed to carry up to 10 passengers, offering a spacious and comfortable cabin. This aircraft is ideal for corporate travel and private travel, providing ample space for all passengers and their luggage.

Power

The LP-5 is equipped with two turbojet engines with a total capacity of 9,000 pounds. These engines provide high speed and reliability, allowing the aircraft to reach cruising speeds of up to 850 km/h and cover distances of up to 5,500 km. Powerful engines ensure stable operation and safety even in difficult flight conditions.

Interior design

The interior of the LP-5 is made using premium materials and modern technologies. The spacious interior is equipped with comfortable leather chairs with the possibility of adjustment, which allows passengers to find the optimal position for maximum comfort. The aircraft is equipped with a noise reduction system and high-quality sound insulation, ensuring a quiet and peaceful atmosphere on board.

Jet SW-10

Capacity

The Jet SW-10 is designed to carry up to 12 passengers, making it an ideal choice for large delegations and long-haul intercontinental flights. The spacious cabin provides comfortable accommodation for all passengers and their luggage, which is especially important for long-distance travel.

Power

The Jet SW-10 is equipped with four turbojet engines with a total capacity of 16,000 pounds. Interior design
