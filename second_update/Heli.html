Heli GF-2

Capacity

The GF-2 business helicopter is designed for comfortable transportation of up to 6 passengers, which makes it an excellent choice for business and private trips. The compact but spacious cabin provides enough space for convenient accommodation of passengers and their luggage.

Power

The GF-2 is equipped with a single 900 horsepower turboshaft engine. This powerful engine provides high performance and reliability, allowing the helicopter to reach a cruising speed of up to 250 km/h and reach a flight range of up to 700 km. The efficient engine design guarantees economical fuel consumption and minimal emissions.

Heli TR-7

Capacity

The TR-7 is designed to carry up to 8 passengers, offering a spacious and comfortable cabin. This helicopter is ideal for corporate travel and private travel, providing ample space for all passengers and their luggage.

Power

The TR-7 is equipped with two turboshaft engines with a total capacity of 1,800 horsepower. These engines provide high speed and reliability, allowing the helicopter to reach a cruising speed of up to 280 km/h and reach a flight range of up to 800 km. The dual engine system improves flight safety and stability in all weather conditions.
