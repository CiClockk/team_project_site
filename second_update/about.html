About Us

The main area of activity

Our company specializes in providing aircraft rental and sale services, including business jets and helicopters. We offer a wide range of models that meet the different needs and preferences of our customers — from standard to the most premium.

The company's fleet

The following categories of aircraft are represented in our fleet:

Standard models:

These models are designed for customers who are looking for reliable and cost-effective solutions for their flights. Standard business jets and helicopters are suitable for short and medium flights, providing a basic level of comfort and functionality.

Premium models:

Premium aircraft are designed for those who appreciate the highest level of comfort, luxury and exclusivity. These models are equipped with the latest technology, spacious interiors, and provide maximum comfort for passengers.

Our advantages

A wide selection: Our fleet includes aircraft for every taste and budget — from economical models to luxury airliners.

Individual approach: We take into account all the wishes of our customers and offer solutions that best meet their requirements.

High Quality: All our aircraft undergo strict quality control and regular maintenance.

Professionalism: Our team consists of experienced specialists who are ready to provide advice and assistance at every stage — from choosing a model to completing a transaction.

Our services

Aircraft rental: We offer flexible rental terms, including short- and long-term rentals of business jets and helicopters.

Aircraft sales: Both new and used models that have been thoroughly tested can be purchased from us.

Maintenance and support: We provide a full cycle of maintenance and support to ensure the safe and uninterrupted operation of aircraft.

And Conclusion

Our company strives to provide its customers with the best conditions for comfortable and safe flights. Whether you are looking for a standard business travel solution or an exclusive liner for special occasions, we are always ready to offer you the best options and a personalized approach. All the best for you!
